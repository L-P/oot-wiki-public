Broken Deku Stick

glitch, child-only

Broken Deku Stick # Description # Broken Deku Stick is a glitch that allows using a broken Deku Stick after breaking it, meaning you can indefinitely and rapidly output Master Sword level damage as child without ever having to obtain the Kokiri Sword. The glitch occurs when a Deku Stick hits two times in a single jump-slash and will stay activated until the item in Link’s hand is replaced or a Scene transition occurs. ...
