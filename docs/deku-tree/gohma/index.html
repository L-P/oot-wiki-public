Gohma One Cycle

Description

This one-cycle allows you to defeat Gohma in under five seconds.

Setup

This setup requires two Deku Sticks one of which will be consumed, and one Deku Nut.

  1. After getting through the door, perform the Broken Deku Stick glitch on a wall.
  2. After Gohma drops to the floor, go closer to it and throw a Deku Nut with your C-Buttons
  3. Power Crouch Stab once
  4. While the animation of the previous attack is still playing, start spamming your C-Buttons to throw another Deku Nut. If you are successful in doing so, Gohma will make a sound.
  5. Power Crouch Stab twice more.

Three hits are enough to kill Gohma, if you fail to hit the second Deku Nut, you may still be able to perform the one cycle but it is inconsistent. If Gohma starts to get away you may have enough time to throw another Deku Nut and kill before it climbs to the ceiling.
